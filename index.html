Assistant Professor of Finance

Whitman School of Management at Syracuse University

Biography

Candace Jens is an assistant professor of finance at the Whitman School of Management at Syracuse University. Her research interests include firm investment and financing policies, how political uncertainty and connections affect firms, panel data econometrics, and the adaptation of machine learning techniques for causal inference.
